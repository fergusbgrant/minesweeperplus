instructions

for the most part, minesweeper+ is played in the same way as classic minesweeper. however, in order to offset the occurrence of logical impasses and subsequent gambles during the game, a new mechanic has been added to gameplay.

minesweeper+ has a 'reveal' mode to assist the player when they encounter such impasses. with this mode, the player has a number of times (difficulty dependent) in each game that they can skip a 50/50 gamble by revealing what is under two adjacent tiles.

to toggle reveal mode, press the space bar during gameplay; the background colour of the page's reveal count will change back and forth to show that the mode has switched. once reveal mode is engaged, click on any tile, and that tile and its adjacent tiles will be highlighted in pink. After this, click on the adjacent tile you wish to reveal along with the tile that was initially clicked; both will be revealed, and the game will return to normal mode.

if, after engaging reveal mode, you click on the wrong tile, you can simply hit the space bar again to clear your selection and exit reveal mode.
